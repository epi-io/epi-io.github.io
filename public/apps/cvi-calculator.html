        About this Calculator

        Validity Index Calculator

        This tool computes Content Validity Index (I-CVI, S-CVI/Ave, S-CVI/UA), Face Validity Index (I-FVI, S-FVI/Ave, S-FVI/UA), and Lawshe's Content Validity Ratio (CVR) entirely in your browser — no data is uploaded to any server.

        References

        Yusoff MSB. ABC of content validation and content validity index calculation. Education in Medicine Journal. 2019;11(2):49–54.

        Yusoff MSB. ABC of response process validation and face validity index calculation. Education in Medicine Journal. 2019;11(3):55–61.

        Lawshe CH. A quantitative approach to content validity. Personnel Psychology. 1975;28:563–575.
